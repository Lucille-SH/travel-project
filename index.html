Why Youghal?

Youghal, the town of timeless charm

Youghal, nestled along the picturesque coastline of County Cork, Ireland, proudly stands as the most easterly town in the county and is a true gem in Ireland's Ancient East. Renowned for its very unique history and natural beauty, Youghal seamlessly blends a modern town center with tangible historic character, creating an atmosphere of curiosity around every corner.

The town boasts a rich tapestry of historical landmarks, including the iconic Clock Gate and the medieval St. Mary's Collegiate Church. Moreover, Youghal's charm extends beyond its streets, with miles of Blue Flag beaches and waterside amenities that give the town the allure of a seaside suburb.

This perfect blend of heritage and coastal allure makes Youghal a destination where the past and present harmoniously coexist, offering a delightful experience for locals and visitors alike.
